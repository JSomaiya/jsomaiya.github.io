About Me

Hi, I'm Jai. I am currently working as a Lecturer in Computer Science at Leicester College. I have been working in this area for the past 4 years.

I have taught wide range of subjects from Spreadsheets to Programming and Cyber Security Theories. I am very adaptable to many different subjects within the Computer Science field.

I have experience in website design and development as well as programming in different languages. I have a small amount of experience in managing a team and am self-motivated to improve my skills.

I define myself by the work I want to do rather than work I have done. Skills can be taught, personality is inherent. I prefer to keep learning and continue to challenge myself, and do intresting things that matter.
